About Me

Born and raised in Costa Rica, Rebeca is a singer/songwriter/worship leader that has recorded in several Spanish Christian albums and toured for the VineyardMusic label.

She is married and has two beautiful daughters.

Rebeca enjoys computers and design. She started her own business in 1996 for web/graphic design that converted into IT support for business in 1998, now with the name of LiveConnexus.

Solving puzzles and fixing things are her passion. Fixing things from a microwave, dishwasher, etc to computer and bug fixes, make her feel so accomplished.

She enjoys designing and creating solutions to life problems through software.
